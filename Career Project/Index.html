Average Salary?

The average salary for a tax accountant is $55,100.

Typical Day In This Career?

Tax accountants handle quarterly payments, payroll, and different kinds of taxes. You may work with a team or be independent.

Companies who hire for this career?

Almost every business needs a tax accountant. A tax accountant may work for a corporate accounting office,public accounting firm, or an independent CPA.
